What We Offer Teams

Comprehensive Neurology Support for Athletics

From protocol development to sideline decisions to staff training — we partner with your organization to ensure every athlete gets evidence-based concussion care.

Return-to-Play Protocol Development

We help your organization create and implement evidence-based RTP protocols aligned with Amsterdam Consensus guidelines, customized for your sports and your staff's capabilities.

  • Protocol design for your specific sports
  • Integration with existing medical workflows
  • Documentation and tracking systems
  • Staff alignment and sign-off

Sideline Assessment & Medical Staff Support

Same-day or next-day evaluation of suspected concussions. Available for events, on-call for emergencies, and present at key competitions to provide immediate expert assessment.

  • On-site concussion evaluation
  • Immediate return-to-play decision
  • Real-time medical staff consultation
  • Event coverage for competitions

Medical Staff Training & Education

Empower your athletic trainers, coaches, and medical staff with the latest concussion recognition, assessment, and management protocols backed by current science.

  • Sideline concussion recognition training
  • Baseline testing coordination
  • Return-to-play protocol workshops
  • Annual continuing education updates

Retained Team Neurologist Advisory

Ongoing retainer relationship for high-stakes decisions, complex cases, and season-long support. Have direct access to neurology expertise when you need it.

  • Available for consultation calls
  • Case review and second opinions
  • Return-to-play clearance decisions
  • Annual protocol review and updates

Baseline Testing & Coordination

Help coordinate neuropsychological baseline testing (ImPACT, CNS Vital Signs, etc.) for your athlete population before injuries occur.

Policy Development & Risk Management

Design concussion policies aligned with state law, insurance requirements, and best practices in athletic safety and liability management.
